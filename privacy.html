Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2026 Last Updated: May 9, 2026

Plain English Summary: CoverEDU is for parents and guardians only. We connect to your Gmail — with your permission — to find school emails and track academic deadlines for your children. We do not sell your data, show ads, or share your information with marketers. You can delete everything at any time from within the app.

1. Who We Are

CoverEDU ("we," "our," or "us") is a mobile application developed by Atriox-lir, an independent software developer based in the United States. CoverEDU is available on iOS and Android and is designed exclusively for parents and legal guardians to monitor school-related emails and academic deadlines for their children.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

2. Who Can Use CoverEDU — Adults Only

CoverEDU accounts are for adults aged 18 and older only. You must be a parent or legal guardian to create an account. Children and minors do not create CoverEDU accounts — they are represented as student profiles managed by the account-holding parent or guardian. CoverEDU does not knowingly collect personal information directly from any person under 18.

If we learn that a person under 18 has created an account directly, we will promptly delete that account and all associated data. If you believe this has occurred, please contact us immediately using the details in Section 13.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Your name: Collected during onboarding to personalize your account. Stored in our cloud backend.
  • Your Google account: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email address and a unique Google user ID for authentication. We do not receive or store your Google account password.
  • Student profiles: You enter the first name and school sender email address(es) for each child you want to track. We do not collect student email addresses, school IDs, student grades, or any official student records.

3.2 Gmail Data We Access

When you connect your Gmail account, CoverEDU requests read-only access. We use this access only to:

  • Scan your inbox for emails from school senders you have configured.
  • Extract the sender address, subject line, date received, and message ID of matching emails to display them in the app and generate deadline reminders.

What we never store from Gmail: Full email body text, email attachments, and emails from senders outside your configured school list. Email body content is processed only on your device to detect deadlines and is never uploaded to our servers.

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Push notification token: An Expo push token used to deliver deadline and new-email notifications to your device.
  • Subscription status: Your plan type (free trial, free, or premium), managed via RevenueCat and your app store account. We do not receive or store your payment card information.

3.4 Google OAuth Refresh Token

To sync school emails when your phone is asleep, we may store a Google OAuth refresh token on our secure backend. This token is:

  • Encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM encryption.
  • Used exclusively to fetch new matching school email metadata on your behalf.
  • Deleted immediately and permanently when you disconnect your Google account or delete your CoverEDU account.
  • Never shared with any third party for any purpose.

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

CoverEDU is designed with children's privacy as a priority. Our approach to COPPA compliance:

  • Parents are the account holders. Only the parent or guardian creates an account and provides consent. Children do not interact with CoverEDU directly and do not provide us any information.
  • Student data is minimal. We store only a child's first name (as a display label) and school sender email domain — both entered by the parent. We do not collect any other student information.
  • No advertising or profiling. We do not use student profile data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or any purpose other than organizing the parent's view within the app.
  • Parental control. Parents can review, edit, or delete their child's profile at any time in Settings → Manage Students, or by deleting their account entirely.
  • No direct collection from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information directly, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

CoverEDU's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • Gmail data is used only to provide the email tracking and deadline features you have configured.
  • We do not use Gmail data to develop, improve, or train AI or machine learning models.
  • We do not allow any person to read your Gmail data except as required for security, compliance, or with your explicit consent.
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties except as necessary to provide CoverEDU's service (our infrastructure providers listed in Section 7) or as required by law.
  • We do not use Gmail data for any purpose unrelated to providing CoverEDU's features to you.

7. How We Share Your Information

We share your data only with service providers necessary to operate CoverEDU:

  • Google Firebase: Backend infrastructure for authentication, cloud database, and notification delivery. Data is stored on Firebase servers (primarily US-based) subject to Google's data processing agreements. Google Privacy Policy →
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. We share your app store account identifier to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy →
  • Expo (Expo Inc.): Push notification delivery. Expo receives your push token and notification content. Expo Privacy Policy →

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority. In the event of a business acquisition or merger, we will notify you before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

8. Data Storage and Retention

Where Your Data Lives

  • On your device: Email display content, student profiles, settings, and your local email cache — in the app's secure local storage.
  • Firebase (Google Cloud, US): Cloud backup of student names, email metadata index (sender, subject, date), app settings, and plan state.
  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Encrypted Google refresh tokens for background sync (only if you enable this feature).

How Long We Keep It

  • Email metadata: Up to 180 days by default (adjustable in app settings), then automatically deleted.
  • Account and student profile data: Retained while your account is active; permanently deleted when you delete your account.
  • Google refresh token: Deleted immediately when you disconnect Google or delete your account.
  • Push notification token: Deleted when your account is deleted.

9. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request a summary of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update your name or student profiles within the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete your account in Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data from our servers.
  • Revoke Gmail Access: Disconnect Google in Settings → Google Connection, or directly at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Notification Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in Settings or your device's system settings.
  • Do Not Sell: We do not sell your personal information. No opt-out needed.

To make any privacy request, contact us (Section 13). We respond within 45 days.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to delete your personal information, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as defined under California law.

10. Security

We implement the following safeguards:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for OAuth refresh tokens stored server-side.
  • Firebase Security Rules restricting all data access to the authenticated account owner — no other user or employee can read your data.
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Secure local storage using platform-provided mechanisms on your device.

No system is completely secure. If you suspect a security issue or account compromise, contact us immediately.
